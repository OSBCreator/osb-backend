Platform Safety Index

We rate the platforms
where scams begin.

OSB assesses every major dating and social platform on its fraud prevention measures. Romance scammers find victims on these platforms. The platforms have a responsibility. OSB holds them to account.

Why This Matters

Platforms create the conditions.
OSB measures the responsibility.

Romance scammers do not operate in a vacuum. They operate on platforms that profit from user engagement — including engagement manufactured by scammers.

The OSB Platform Safety Index is the world's first systematic assessment of dating and social platforms on their anti-romance-fraud measures. Each platform is rated on five criteria and given an OSB Safety Score out of 100.

This index will be updated annually. Platforms that improve will see their scores rise. Platforms that do not will remain on record.

OSB Assessment Criteria
01
Identity Verification
Does the platform require users to verify who they are before contacting others?
02
AI Fraud Detection
Does the platform use automated tools to detect romance scam behaviour?
03
Abuse Reporting
How fast and effective is response to fraud reports?
04
Scam-Specific Tools
Are there tools specifically built to detect and stop romance fraud?
05
User Education
Does the platform actively warn users about romance fraud risk?
Grading: A (80–100) · B (65–79) · C (50–64) · D (35–49) · F (0–34)
