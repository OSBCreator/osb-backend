The Framework

Four pillars.
One score. Zero ambiguity.

The RSEI Model™ is the world's only behavioural threat detection system built for social engineering fraud. Four detection modules. One Core RSEI Rating. Every exploitation follows the same behavioural sequence — RSEI detects it.

Read the full framework →
R
Pillar 01
1—5
Relationship Acceleration
How fast the scammer engineers false intimacy, emotional dependency and premature declarations of love and commitment.
S
Pillar 02
1—5
Script Consistency
The rehearsed persona — occupation, location, backstory and financial credibility props designed to feel verifiable.
E
Pillar 03
1—5
Emotional Manipulation
Exploitation of trust, guilt, love and fear. E ≥ 4 triggers mandatory support — regardless of financial loss.
I
Pillar 04
1—5
Incremental Extraction
Escalating financial asks — mule recruitment, gift cards, wire transfers, cryptocurrency. Small begins. Large follows.
E≥4
E Pillar Independent Trigger — Active at E ≥ 4: OSB mandates victim support referral regardless of total Core Score and regardless of whether any financial loss has occurred. Emotional harm is real harm. This trigger is unique to the RSEI Model™ and was established through field case IFN-002.
Risk Classification

Three tiers.
No ambiguity. No softening.

4
4—11
Low Behavioural Risk
Patterns present but below confirmed fraud threshold. Monitoring required. Stay alert — particularly around financial requests and inability to verify identity in person.
Protocol: Monitor · Document · Stay alert to escalation
12
12—16
High Probability Romance Scam
Multiple confirmed fraud indicators present. Hallmarks of a professional, organised operation. Do not send money. Screenshot all evidence. Contact OSB now.
Protocol: Do not send money · Screenshot · Contact OSB
17
17—20
Critical Exploitation
Advanced exploitation in active progress. Immediate action required. Stop all financial transactions. Secure your evidence. Contact your bank and OSB without delay.
Protocol: Stop all transactions · Contact bank · Contact OSB immediately
