About OSB

Built to fight.
Not to observe.

OSB was not built to raise awareness. It was built to act — with intelligence, precision and the only behavioural framework of its kind in the world.

Who We Are

The Bureau

OSB is a behavioural intelligence organisation founded in 2021 under the trading name OSB. OSB exists for one purpose: to identify, document and stop romance fraud using a scientific, evidence-based framework.

We are not a charity. We are not a government agency. We are an intelligence operation — built on the conviction that the patterns of romance fraud can be named, measured and defeated.

OSB operates primarily on Quora under the handle John Dee. Combined reach exceeds 20,700+ views. Our content has intervened in active scam situations in real time — including documented cases where people exited ongoing scams after engaging with OSB work.

The Difference

The world scores crimes.
We score behaviour.

Every other fraud awareness organisation reacts. They document losses after the fact. OSB does something different — we measure what is happening right now, before the money is lost. The RSEI Model™ gives any person a concrete, evidence-based score.

Intelligence over opinion
Every OSB position is grounded in documented field evidence — not assumptions or anecdote.
Action over awareness
OSB intervenes in real time, documents what we see, and builds tools that create immediate clarity.
Compassion without compromise
Targets are targeted by professionals. OSB never blames and never softens the truth.
Anonymity as integrity
OSB operates anonymously by design. The work matters — not the person behind it.
