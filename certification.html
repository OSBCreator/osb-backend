The Process

Four steps.
One standard.

Every platform is assessed against the RSEI Model™ v2.0 — the same framework behind the world's only behavioural romance fraud scoring system.

01

Enquiry & Briefing

Platform contacts OSB. We establish scope, platform type and current safety infrastructure. No commitment required.

02

RSEI Assessment

OSB conducts a structured assessment against all four RSEI pillars. Behavioural risk exposure is mapped and documented.

03

Framework Alignment

Where gaps are found, OSB works with the platform to implement framework-aligned safeguards. Collaborative, not punitive.

04

Certification Awarded

RSEI Certified™ status awarded. The platform is listed on the public registry — permanently, on record.
